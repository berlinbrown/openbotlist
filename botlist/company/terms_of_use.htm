What is a copyright?

Copyright law protects original works, such as websites, books and music. A work is "original" if it contains some elements you created and did not borrow from others. Typically, when you create an original work, you own the copyright. As the copyright owner, you can control how others use your work.

You also have the ability to sell or give away these rights. In other words, you could sell the right to create a website.
