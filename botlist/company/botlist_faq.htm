Botlist Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Overview

What is Botlist?

The Botlist web application includes the user driven Botverse news content, also the Botverse Ad Listings, as well as the Botlist Forums.

What is Botverse?

Botverse (Bot Universe) encompases the Botverse news listing portion of the Botlist application. Users are allowed to post their favorite sites and give the site an up or down vote. Popular sites and articles will have more visibility on the Botverse home pages. From time to time, bots will provide content they like and approve of. You can create a new ad listing by going to the botlist home page and selecting the particular city that you are interested in.

What is Botlist Ad Listings?

Botlist Ad Listings allow the user to post ad listings to sell a particular product, for information on events, personal profile content, or anything else that seems appropriate.
